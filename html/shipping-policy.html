Shipping Policy

We deliver digital services, including online accounts for various platforms, which are delivered instantly upon purchase. Please note that as we sell digital services, there is no physical shipping required, and all orders are delivered electronically via email.

If you have any questions or concerns about your order or the delivery process, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@accbuddy.com. We are happy to help!
