Cookies Policy

AccBuddy uses cookies on its website to improve and personalize your experience, as well as for advertising and analytical purposes. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How AccBuddy uses cookies

Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for our website to function. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website.

Performance cookies

These are used to recognize and count the number of visitors to our website and to see how visitors move around our site. This helps us improve the way our website works.

Functionality cookies

These are used to recognize you when you return to our website. This enables us to personalize our content for you, greet you by name, and remember your preferences.

Advertising cookies

These cookies record your visit to our website, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We use this information to make our website and the advertising displayed on it more relevant to your interests.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that if you choose to disable cookies, certain parts of our website may not function correctly.

Changes to our Cookie Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to be informed of how we are using cookies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or this policy, you can contact us at support@accbuddy.com.

By using our website and agreeing to this policy, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with the terms of this policy.
