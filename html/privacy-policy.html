Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how AccBuddy collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website and purchase our online accounts.

What Information We Collect

When you visit our website or make a purchase, we may collect the following types of personal information

Name and Contact Information

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information that you provide to us.

Purchase History

We may collect information about your purchase history on our website.

Device Information

We may collect information about the device you use to access our website, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

Cookies

We may use cookies to collect information about your use of our website.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

Order Processing

We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders for our online accounts.

Communication

We may use your personal information to communicate with you about your orders, to send you updates about our products and services, and to respond to your inquiries.

Website Improvement

We may use your personal information to improve our website and to personalize your experience.

Fraud Prevention

We may use your personal information to prevent fraud and protect our business and our customers.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption and other security measures to protect your information.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as account creation and customer service. We may also share your personal information to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and the rights of our customers.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information that we have collected. You can contact us at support@accbuddy.com or through our contact form to request access to your personal information or to update or delete your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting a notice on our website or by sending you an email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@accbuddy.com or through our contact form.

Consent

By using our website and purchasing our products, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
